Account Deletion

Updated at 2026-03-30

This page explains how users of the Sprrintly app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data, and how such data is handled following a deletion request. This policy is consistent with our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

How to Request Account Deletion

Account deletion can be requested directly within the Sprrintly app. Follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Sprrintly app and log in to your account
  2. On the bottom navigation bar, tap the Organise screen
  3. Navigate to the Profile screen
  4. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner
  5. Tap More
  6. Select Delete Account
  7. Authenticate when prompted — enter your password if required
  8. Tap Delete to confirm

Once the request is confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion. You will receive a confirmation notification upon completion.

Scope of Data Deletion

Upon successful processing of an account deletion request, Sprrintly will permanently delete or anonymize the following data associated with your account:

  • Personal identifiers — including your name and email address
  • User-generated content — including tasks, notes, weekly plans, and activity data
  • Account preferences and application settings

Where full deletion is not immediately possible (for example, due to backup retention cycles), data will be anonymized within the same timeframe so that it can no longer be linked to you.

Data Retention and Exceptions

Certain data may be retained after an account deletion request under the following circumstances. All retained data is stored only for as long as strictly necessary.

Reason Description Retention period
Legal obligations Where retention is required to comply with applicable laws or regulatory requirements in your region (e.g. Australian Privacy Act, GDPR, CCPA) As required by applicable law
Security & fraud prevention To detect, prevent, or address misuse of the service Up to 30 days from deletion request

Unless a longer period is required by law, retained data will generally not be held beyond 30 days from the date of the deletion request. Retained data is limited in scope and will not be used for any other purpose.

Third-Party Authentication

Sprrintly uses third-party authentication providers, including Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In (via Firebase Authentication).

Deleting your Sprrintly account will remove your data from Sprrintly's systems. However, authentication credentials and account access managed by these third-party providers are governed by their own privacy policies and remain under their control. To fully revoke access, you may manage connected apps via your Google or Apple account settings.

Contact and Support

If you are unable to access your account or require assistance completing an account deletion request, you can contact our support team directly. We respond to account deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable data protection requirements, including the Australian Privacy Act, GDPR, and CCPA.

Send your request to:

support@sprrintly.com

Please include the email address associated with your account so we can locate and process your request promptly.
